How to Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your account:

  1. Log in to the app
  2. Visit the Account Settings
  3. Press "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm with the steps in the popup modal

You will then be returned to the login screen, with the app confirming that the account has been deleted.

Please contact us if you are unsure or require confirmation that your account has been deleted.
